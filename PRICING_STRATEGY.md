## ⚠️ Pricing Mistakes to Avoid

### ❌ Don't
- Increase price too fast (wait for reviews)
- Decrease price (signals low quality)
- Offer too many discounts (devalues product)
- Ignore customer feedback
- Forget to announce increases

### ✅ Do
- Wait for sales/reviews before increasing
- Announce increases in advance
- Offer limited-time discounts (not permanent)
- Listen to customer feedback
- Communicate transparently

## 📞 FAQ

**Q: When should I increase price?**
A: After 20 sales or 10 reviews, whichever comes first.

**Q: Will higher price hurt sales?**
A: Not if you have reviews. Reviews = credibility = can charge more.

**Q: Should I offer discounts?**
A: Limited-time only (e.g., "20% off this week"). Not permanent.

**Q: What if sales drop after price increase?**
A: Normal. Some buyers only buy at low prices. Focus on quality over quantity.

**Q: Can I go back to $15?**
A: Not recommended. Signals low quality. Better to offer limited-time discount.
